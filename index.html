My Practice Method

Posted on November 15th, 2023

My current method of practicing a lot more coding is by utilizing both KhanAcademy and FreeCodeCamp while going through the course. I feel that while both websites are great at teaching, there are parts that I feel that one does better than the other and vice-versa, which made me decide that both would be necessary to make swift progress.

KhanAcademy excells at easy-to-learn lessons and digestable bite-sized courses that make it easy to start, regardless of your lack of coding knowledge. This makes it a great place to gather information and enhance your knowledge of the fundamentals of coding.

This is in stark contract to FreeCodeCamp which I personally feel has a less hand-holding method of teaching. This can sometimes lead to obtuse requests without any explanation of the metholodgy being used, thus leading to confusion. However, the adundance of lessons and constant push to help you gain more in-depth knowledge is outstanding.

This is the reason that I feel that both combined is THE WAY to learn coding as a beginner. With both really adding to the experience and helping with retaining information.
